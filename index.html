Frequently Asked Questions

How does Clauding ensure quality freelancers?

We use a multi-step verification process including skill tests, portfolio review, and client feedback. Only top 15% applicants are approved.

What are the fees for using Clauding?

Freelancers pay 10% service fee. Clients pay 5% processing fee. No hidden charges. First project free for new users.

How do I get started as a freelancer?

Sign up, complete your profile, take skill assessments, and start bidding on projects. Our AI will recommend relevant opportunities.

Is payment secure on Clauding?

Yes! We use escrow system. Clients deposit funds before work starts. Freelancers get paid upon milestone completion.

Can I hire for long-term contracts?

Absolutely! Clauding supports hourly, fixed-price, and retainer-based contracts. Perfect for ongoing collaborations.

What makes Clauding different from Upwork or Fiverr?

AI-powered matching, lower fees, faster hiring, focus on tech/IT domain, and direct connection building without bidding wars.

Do you offer customer support?

24/7 support via chat, email, and phone. Premium users get dedicated account managers.

Can I work with international clients/freelancers?

Yes! Clauding is global. We support 150+ countries with multi-currency payments and timezone-friendly scheduling.
